MY Journey into Tech

From Curiosity to Coding

Meet Rachel,a young woman who has recently decided to embark on a new and exciting journey into the world of Technology and coding. She has always been curious about how websites and applications
worked, but never imagined she would learn how to build them herself.

The First Steps

Rachel began by learning the basics of HTML, the foundation of web development. She created her first web page, adding headings, paragraphs, and even formatting her text using bold, Italic, and underline
elements.

Every new tag she learned brought a sense of accomplishent
She quickly became comfortable with HTML elements and is eager to explore more.
Next on her list: learning more HTML elements and CSS to style her web pages!

Challenges and Growth

The journey wasn't always easy. Rachel encountered erros, bugs and challenges that tested her patience. But she never gave up. She knew that with every mistake she was one step closer to becoming a confident coder.

At the end of this program, Rachel can build simple web pages and will slowly venture into java script to make her project interactive.

The Road Ahead

Rachel knows that the road to mastering tech is long, but she is excited for the future. She's even started sharing her progress on social media to inspire others, especially other women, to dive into the world of
coding.

Conclusion

Rachel's journey is just beginnig. With passion, determination and the ability to never stop learning, she knows that the possibilities in tech are endless.
